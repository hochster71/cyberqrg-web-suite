Responsible Disclosure

We appreciate security researchers who help us keep our platform secure. Report vulnerabilities responsibly.

Our Commitment

CyberQRG is committed to security research and welcomes reports from security researchers. We believe that coordinated disclosure of security vulnerabilities strengthens the security of our platform and the broader cybersecurity community.

Guidelines for Researchers

  • Do No Harm: Avoid actions that could disrupt services or compromise user data
  • Coordinate Disclosure: Give us time to fix issues before public disclosure
  • Respect Privacy: Do not access or modify user data without explicit permission
  • Be Professional: Conduct research ethically and professionally

Scope

Our responsible disclosure program covers:

  • cyberqrgai.com and all subdomains
  • Sentinel platform (sentinel.cyberqrgai.com)
  • Privacy tools (privacy.cyberqrgai.com)
  • API endpoints and integrations
  • Mobile applications (when available)

Out of scope:

  • Third-party services and integrations
  • Physical security of facilities
  • Social engineering attacks
  • Denial of service attacks

How to Report

Send vulnerability reports to security@cyberqrgai.com with the following information:

  • Detailed description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Potential impact and severity assessment
  • Your contact information for follow-up
  • Any proof-of-concept code (if safe to share)

Our Process

  1. Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
  2. Assessment: Our security team will assess the report within 7 days
  3. Updates: We will provide regular updates on our progress
  4. Resolution: We will work to fix validated vulnerabilities promptly
  5. Disclosure: Coordinated public disclosure after fixes are deployed

Rewards

We offer rewards for valid vulnerability reports based on severity:

  • Critical: $5,000 - $10,000
  • High: $2,000 - $5,000
  • Medium: $500 - $2,000
  • Low: $100 - $500

Legal Protection

Security researchers who follow these guidelines will not face legal action for their research activities. We will not pursue legal action against researchers who comply with this policy.

Contact

For questions about this program or to report a vulnerability:

Email: security@cyberqrgai.com
