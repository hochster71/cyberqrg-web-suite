Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to our mission. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our cybersecurity services and improve our platform. We minimize data collection to only what is required for our services.

Service Data

  • Security Logs: System and security event logs for threat detection and analysis
  • Configuration Data: Security policies and system configurations you provide
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated usage statistics to improve service performance

Account Information

  • Contact Details: Name, email, and organization information for account management
  • Authentication Data: Credentials and multi-factor authentication tokens
  • Access Logs: Login attempts and session information for security monitoring

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely for providing and improving our cybersecurity services.

  • Security Monitoring: Detect and respond to security threats
  • Service Delivery: Provide cybersecurity analysis and protection
  • Platform Improvement: Enhance service reliability and features
  • Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Communication: Send service updates and security notifications

Data Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls with principle of least privilege
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Data Minimization: Only collect data necessary for service delivery
  • Retention Limits: Data retained only as long as necessary

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in service delivery (under strict contracts)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Security Incidents: Sharing threat intelligence to improve collective security

Your Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@cyberqrgai.com.
